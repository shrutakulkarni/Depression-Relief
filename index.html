What approach should be taken?

  • Online research reveals common solutions for depression: making friends, building a support network, eating healthy, and walking.
  • People with depression often know these solutions but find them hard to implement.
  • Talking and sharing feelings can be more effective.
  • It's okay if you feel like no one understands; try reaching out to strangers or those with similar experiences.
  • Use chat options to discuss your problems.
  • If a friend is depressed, instead of giving advice, listen to them and understand their struggles.
